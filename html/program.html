PROGRAM

When you engage our Camp for your alpine experience, your guide will not just lead you to the summit and back, but also teach you the essential skills required to climb efficiently in the mountains.

The alpine experience of climbing together, often for a full week and staying in mountain huts, develops friendships that last a lifetime.

The personal relationship which is at the heart of the guided alpine experience is enhanced by your guide having the same language, cultural values and mountaineering background as you.

PROGRAM

We have a history of traveling out through Alps, Caucasus, Ands and Himalayans. Many guides now also live all year round in the European Alps but we maintain the flexibility to move around, finding the route that's right for our clients.

The Mountaineers offers courses, clinics, and seminars year round. Whether you’re looking for an introductory seminar or an intensive course, our instructional programs can help you get outside. With seven branches in western Washington, we’ll teach you outdoor skills close to enjoy the PNW and beyond.


Plan ahead using our course calendar. It take a few months for planning, scheduling, conditioning, and registration. Enrollment usually opens two or three months before your first session. Many of our course are taught in the season before you'd typically participate in the activity. For example, we teach sea kayaking in the spring so you'll be ready to paddle in the summer.


Already have the skills? If you have the skills of a course graduate or better, most courses offer an equivalency process for demonstrating those skills. Click on the outdoor skill to find brief overviews of our primary course, clinic, and seminar offerings along with links to more details about the branches who offer our programs, badges you can earn, and obtaining equivalency.
